## Steps

- Create an Oauth app at: https://app.netlify.com/user/applications/new
- Specify environment variables from created OAuth app:
  - `NETLIFY_OAUTH_CLIENT_ID`
  - `NETLIFY_OAUTH_CLIENT_SECRET`
